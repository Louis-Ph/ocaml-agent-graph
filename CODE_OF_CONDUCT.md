# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects technically rigorous, respectful collaboration.

Participants should:

- debate ideas directly without attacking people
- keep criticism specific, factual, and relevant to the work
- assume good faith while still insisting on clarity, evidence, and structure
- respect different experience levels, backgrounds, and communication styles

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- personal attacks, insults, or repeated bad-faith escalation
- doxxing, threats, or publication of private information
- disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder without improving the work

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests,
review comments, and other project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
privately through GitHub. For security vulnerabilities, use the process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or ban
participants whose behavior violates this policy.
